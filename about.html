My Story

Hi, I'm Gareth. I'm a product designer with a background in sound design. I've always loved puzzling my way through tricky problems, and that, combined with my experience in design, led me to pursue a career in UX. My past work in sound design for theater allows me to look at user experience through the lens of narrative storytelling and what makes things engaging and understandable. I have been a part of many design projects where I have contributed to creating something from the ground up. I'm excited to continue doing this on UX projects where I can have a personal impact and build something that betters people's lives.
